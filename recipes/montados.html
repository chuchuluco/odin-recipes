Authentic Montados Chihuahua

Montados Chihuahua (cheese quesadilla with beef)

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Chihuahua cheese or mozzarella
  • Pinto beans
  • Beef or your favorite stew

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Heat the handmade tortilla in a comal or frying pan, spread the beans to taste and add enough cheese.

  2. Step 2

    Add your favorite stew and wrap.

If you like you can accompany them with avocado, onion slices and a rich spicy sauce.
